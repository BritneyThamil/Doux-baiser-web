Learn About Our Online Ordering Process

Choose And Pre-Order Order And Shipping Receive, Enjoy And Share
The first step is to select the desserts you want to order, your favorite toppings and the drink of your choice, once you choose the dessert and the quantities you go to the pre-order, where you put your payment information, your address, your name and the information of your order required. Once we obtain the data of your order we will be calling the number left in the information data that you have placed in the previous step, when contacting you we will confirm the data of your order, once this step has been carried out, the only thing left to do is wait. The order will be delivered to the address provided by the customer, once the order has been delivered and the order has been signed, confirming that the order has been delivered in good quality and that there is no problem, all that remains is to settle in and enjoy! you can share with your loved ones and leave us your comment in our mail, we always appreciate the opinions.
